Dune part 2

An adaptation of Frank Herbert's best-selling novel. A very distant future. The desert planet Arrakis, also known as Dune, is the only place in the universe from where melange is extracted - a precious substance that not only prolongs life, but also allows interstellar travel and glimpses into the future. By order of Emperor Shaddam IV, Dune is given as a fief to the noble family of Atreides. Duke Leto Atreides, Lady Jessica and their son, Paul, arrive on Arrakis. The wicked Harkonnen family, however, is not about to give up the planet so easily to their enemies. For whoever rules Dune, rules the entire universe.
